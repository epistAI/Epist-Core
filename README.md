### Revolutionizing Education in Bangladesh with RAG-Powered Generative AI

**Epist** is an elite AI-powered mentorship platform designed to democratize high-quality education for students following the **NCTB (National Curriculum and Textbook Board)** in Bangladesh. 

By leveraging **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)**, Epist provides 24/7, context-aware tutoring that is strictly syllabus-aligned, ensuring students receive accurate academic support without the hallucinations common in generic LLMs.
